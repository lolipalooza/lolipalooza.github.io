Response to Why doesnt NG have no mobile app? 2022-07-22 21:35:32

Some stuff that they've talked about previously if i remember right:


Google doesn't like NG much. NG has been blocked from AdSense since forever. Not a great thing if you want to put an app on the Play Store.


You gotta pay to keep your apps in the app stores and you have to update them regularly. They did have a NG made game for iPhone, Groundcats. When they didn't update it or pay for more years to keep it in the app store it was removed. And now it's gone RIPepperoni


App stores don't allow for apps that are like the Newgrounds Portal for games - they would prefer each game be submitted to their own app stores. 3rd party apps like a Newgrounds one would circumvent their own policies (and pocket books) if creators could just upload to newgrounds for free and everyone can just get it from the Newgrounds app in the app store (I mean, they can just open NG on their browser, but Apple never liked Flash and this was all before Ruffle or NG Player). To conform to the rules of app stores you'd lose a really big part of Newgrounds, the games. They probably would side eye the movies too. Art would probably be OK and the BBS...but that's neutering the main traffic to the site - games.


Any newgrounds app would just end up being a browser type app - you know the ones that it's an 'app' but all it does is load a webpage. A team of like 3 or so guys isn't going to have the time to develop for this whole unwieldy website plus a mobile app that'll be just as complicated. Why bother with that if you can just open it in a browser that's maintained and updated by Google/Apple/Firefox.
